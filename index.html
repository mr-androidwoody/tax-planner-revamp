Setting up your accounts

Each row represents a single account or investment wrapper. Add one row per account — for example, a Vanguard ISA, a workplace pension, and a trading account would each be separate rows.

Wrapper
The tax wrapper this account sits in. ISAs are tax-free. SIPP/WPs are taxed as income on withdrawal (with 25% typically tax-free). GIAs are subject to dividend tax and CGT. Cash accounts earn interest within their structure.
SIPP/Workplace pension
Pension wrappers. Contributions receive tax relief. Withdrawals are typically 25% tax-free and 75% taxed as income. Access is usually restricted until pension age.
ISA (Stocks & Shares)
Fully tax-free wrapper. No tax on income or gains, and no tax on withdrawals. Annual contribution limits apply.
Cash ISA
Tax-free cash savings. Interest is not taxed. Typically used for short-term savings or cash buffers.
GIA (General Investment Account)
No tax shelter. Dividends, interest, and capital gains are taxable, with allowances available. Fully flexible access.
Cash
Standard cash holdings used for liquidity, spending, or short-term reserves rather than long-term growth.
Cashlike
Low-risk, interest-bearing assets such as money market funds. These sit between cash and bonds — typically offering modest returns with low volatility. Often used as a cash buffer within an investment portfolio.
Value (£)
The current market value of this account. This is the starting balance the engine uses — enter what it's worth right now.
Equities % / Bonds % / Cashlike %
How this account is invested. Equities drive growth, bonds provide stability, cashlike assets sit between the two. These should add up to 100% along with Cash %.
Cash %
The proportion held as uninvested cash. Cash earns no investment return — keep this low unless the account is genuinely held as cash.
Rate % AER
For interest-bearing holdings such as savings or cashlike assets. Sets the annual rate the engine uses to calculate income. Leave blank for purely invested accounts.
Draw £/mo
A fixed monthly amount drawn from this account before other wrappers are touched. Useful for modelling a regular income stream from a cash or cashlike pot. Leave blank to let the engine manage withdrawals automatically.
